Frequently Asked Questions

How long does it take to set up?

Most businesses are up and running in less than 15 minutes. The QuickBooks connection takes just a few clicks, and we provide customizable templates to get you started right away.

Is my QuickBooks data secure?

Absolutely. We use OAuth 2.0 for secure authentication and never store your QuickBooks credentials. All data is encrypted both in transit and at rest.

Can I exclude certain customers?

Yes, you can easily exclude specific customers or invoices from the automation. This gives you flexibility for special relationships or unique situations.

How do customers pay their invoices?

Payment processing is handled securely by Intuit Payments, the same trusted payment platform used by QuickBooks. When customers click "Pay Now" in your follow-up emails, they're taken directly to Intuit's secure payment portal to complete their transaction.
